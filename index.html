The Moment
Fireworks & Project Reveal
“The sky opens. The music swells. حياة appears.”
A 15-minute pyrotechnic performance synchronised to original score, culminating in a time-coded light & laser sequence that delivers the project reveal film.
15MIN
Start8:45 PM
End9:00 PM
PlacementPost-Speeches
01
Opening Pyro · 0:00 – 4:30
Aerial shells open the sequence — low, mid, and high tier bursts choreographed to a building orchestral overture. Stage lighting dimmed to silhouette the skyline.
02
Laser & Light Bridge · 4:30 – 10:00
Ground-based lasers and beam lights take over, painting the audience and venue axis. Pyro recedes into rhythmic ground effects. Music transitions to the Hayat theme.
03
Project Reveal Film · 10:00 – 15:00
Time-coded video reveal: the حياة project film plays on the stage LED wall, frame-synced to laser lines across the facade and a final pyro crescendo on the closing shot.

06
Operations
Crowd Management
Capacity & Entry Control
  • Total event capacity: 1,000 seated guests (700 + 300 VIP)
  • 4 entry gates with dedicated VIP fast-track lane
  • Digital QR ticketing & RFID wristbands
  • Real-time occupancy monitoring via counter system
  • Max entry rate: 200 guests / 10 min at peak
Zone Flow Strategy
  • Colour-coded wristbands per zone (General / VIP / Media)
  • One-way flow corridors to avoid counter-flow congestion
  • Barrier system with 6 marshalled checkpoint stations
  • Emergency evacuation routes signposted (4 exits)
  • Separate exit channels to prevent bottlenecks at close

07
Safety
Security & Safety Plan
Security Personnel
150+ trained officers across all zones and backstage. Uniformed and plainclothes units, licensed private security.
Ops Command Center
On-site war room coordinating all security, medical, and event teams. Two-way radio across all units with dedicated supervisor channel.
Bag Check & Screening
Walk-through metal detectors at all gates. X-ray screening at VIP entry. Prohibited items list communicated in advance.
First Aid & Medical
2 medical units with paramedics, ambulance on standby, AED equipment. Coordinated with Mansoura University Hospital.
